* Introduction
  This document deals with the UX/UI and design differences between
  the previous version of published vlabs website with the recently
  released pages. After the inputs from the different institutes and
  users regarding their labs not visible any more on the current
  vlabs.ac.in, an elaborate discussion on the same brought out other
  design differences and the delta of differences seems to have
  widened even more than the previous releases.
